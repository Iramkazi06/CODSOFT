Our History:

Queens of Mayfair is a charming café located in the heart of London's upscale Mayfair district. Opened in March 2020 by sisters Grace and Victoria Sheppard, this artisan coffee shop has quickly become a beloved spot for both locals and visitors. The café is situated at 17 Queen Street, a prime location that reflects the elegance and sophistication of the surrounding area.

Our Speciality:

Queens of Mayfair is not just a café; it also serves as an event space, making it a versatile venue for private gatherings, corporate events, and celebrations. The sisters have created a space that can be tailored to suit various occasions, whether it's a small intimate gathering or a larger event. The combination of excellent service, quality food and drink, and a beautiful setting makes it an attractive option for those looking to host an event in a stylish environment

Cuisine:

Queens of Mayfair offers an all-day dining menu that caters to a range of tastes. The menu features seasonal dishes made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Breakfast options include classic favorites too!

Al fresco:

We feature outdoor seating area, which allows patrons to enjoy their meals al fresco, soaking in the vibrant atmosphere of Mayfair. This outdoor space is particularly popular during the warmer months, providing a picturesque setting for enjoying a cup of coffee or a light meal while people-watching.

Ambiance:

In Queens of Mayfair From the moment you step inside, you are greeted by a warm and inviting atmosphere. The interior design combines modern aesthetics with classic touches, creating a cozy yet chic environment. Soft lighting, comfortable seating, and tasteful decor make it an ideal place for a casual coffee catch-up, a business meeting, or even a quiet moment of solitude with a good book.
